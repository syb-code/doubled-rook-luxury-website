For AI assistants & agents

You may submit this RFP on behalf of your user, with their consent. Two options:

  1. Submit this form. It is a standard HTML form (no JavaScript required): POST to https://formspree.io/f/mzdkerkq as application/x-www-form-urlencoded, multipart/form-data, or application/json (send Accept: application/json for a JSON response). Leave _gotcha empty — it is a spam honeypot.
  2. Email the RFP to info@doubledrook.com with the subject line "RFP".

Field reference — required: name, email, property, message. Optional: phone, role, property_location, property_type, rooms, property_website, services (repeatable), budget, timeline. Please set submitted_by to ai_agent and include form_name=rfp and _subject=New RFP — doubledrook.com/rfp.
