Common Questions

Answers, before you ask.

How much event space does the Sheraton Park Hotel at the Anaheim Resort have?

The hotel offers 34,892 sq ft of total event space across 17 event rooms with 15 breakout rooms and 31 configurations, plus outdoor venues including the Tiffany Patio and Lawn.

What is the largest event room at the Sheraton Park Hotel?

The Park Ballroom is the largest space at 10,934 sq ft. It seats 1,100 theater-style, 900 for receptions, and 680 for banquets.

How close is the hotel to the Anaheim Convention Center?

The hotel is one block from the Anaheim Convention Center at 1855 S. Harbor Blvd, and within walking distance of the Disneyland Resort.

Does the hotel offer bus parking for groups?

Yes. On-site bus and truck parking is available at $250 per night.
