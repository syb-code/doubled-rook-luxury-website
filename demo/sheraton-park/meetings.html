Built for the Anaheim citywide calendar.

NAMM, Natural Products Expo West, WonderCon, VidCon — when a citywide compresses Harbor Blvd, exhibitor and overflow blocks of 10–100 rooms need a hotel that answers fast and sits one block from the doors. That is this hotel.

Citywide overflow & exhibitor blocks

490 rooms one block from the ACC. Blocks of 10–100 rooms with quick turnaround during show compression windows — plus hospitality suites and client-dinner F&B buyouts during show weeks.

Self-contained programs

17 event rooms, 15 breakouts, and a ballroom that seats 1,100 theater / 680 banquet host quarterly trainings, regional sales meetings, and full conferences outright — an easy drive for the Irvine and OC corporate corridor.

Studios & small groups

Sheraton Studio spaces (Studio A & B) and the Promenade give small teams and breakout sessions a modern, flexible setting — conference-set for 6 to 10.

Planner Questions

The details, answered.

How many meeting rooms does the Sheraton Park Hotel at the Anaheim Resort have?

17 event rooms and 15 breakout rooms totaling 34,892 sq ft across 31 configurations, plus the 3,042 sq ft El Prado Foyer for receptions and registration.

What is the capacity of the Park Ballroom?

10,934 sq ft: 1,100 theater, 430 schoolroom, 900 reception, 680 banquet. It divides into eight sections (Park A–H) for programs of 30 to 750.

Does the hotel provide audiovisual services?

Yes — Encore is the on-site AV and production partner, with high-speed internet available throughout the event space.

Do meeting planners earn points?

Yes. Marriott Bonvoy Events awards up to 60,000 points per qualified event; Elite members earn bonus points.
