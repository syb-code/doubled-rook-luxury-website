The Venues

From garden ceremony to 680-guest ballroom.

Park Ballroom

10,934 square feet, fully renovated, seats 680 for a banquet and 900 for a reception. Grand enough for a 500-guest celebration, flexible enough for an intimate one.

Tiffany Patio & Lawn

Outdoor ceremonies and receptions under the palms: 500 on the fountain patio, 600 on the lawn, with the nightly fireworks as your send-off.

Cultural weddings, welcomed

South Asian, Vietnamese, Filipino, and Hispanic weddings of 300–700 guests are a specialty, not an exception. Ask our events team about menu and catering flexibility for your traditions, from sangeet to Tết banquet.

Celebrations year-round

Quinceañeras, milestone birthdays, Lunar New Year and Tết banquets, holiday parties, ballroom-led events with a dedicated on-site culinary team.
