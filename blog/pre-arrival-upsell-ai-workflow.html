Your pre-arrival emails are leaving $40–$80 per guest on the table

May 19, 2026 2 min read By Michael Gomez Agentic AI · Hotel Marketing · Revenue Optimization

Your pre-arrival emails are leaving $40-$80 per guest on the table. Here's the AI workflow that captures it.

Most hotels send a generic "we can't wait to host you" email 7 days before arrival. It's pleasant. It generates almost zero ancillary revenue.

The workflow:

  1. Segmentation agent pulls each upcoming guest and tags them: business vs. leisure, party size, room type, length of stay, repeat vs. new, time-zone-of-origin.
  2. Recommendation agent matches each guest to the highest-conversion upsell offer based on their tags. Examples:

• Couples on weekend stays → spa package (35-45% conversion)

• Business travelers → late checkout + airport transfer bundle (50%+ conversion)

• Repeat guests → room upgrade at 30% off (very high conversion)

• Long-stay guests → laundry service or in-room dining credit

  1. Drafting agent writes the email — pulls property-specific copy, plugs in the right offer, generates a personal subject line, includes a one-click "add to my reservation" link.
  2. Approval agent flags anything weird (offers that exceed rate parity rules, mismatched tone, broken merge fields). Marketing reviews and approves the queue once daily.
  3. Send agent dispatches at the optimal time for each guest's time zone, then logs conversion data back into the recommendation agent for continuous improvement.

A hotel doing 10,000 stays a year with even a 20% upsell take-up at $50 average ancillary = $100,000 in revenue that wasn't there before.

No additional headcount. No new platform. Just better workflow.
