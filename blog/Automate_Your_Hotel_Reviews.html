Automate Your Hotel Reviews

May 6, 2026 2 min read By Michael Gomez

We built an AI agent that automates review responses. Here’s how it works:

  • 1. We created an AI agent that scrapes sites for new reviews (Google and OTAs)
  • 2. A second agent comes in and drafts a response matched to the review’s tone, sentiment, and specific details. The agent pings the team once done.
  • 3. A team member reviews, edits and approves (quick gut check and added personalization)
  • 4. Response publishes

The prompt framework we use:

“You are a guest relations manager at [Hotel Name]. Respond to this review in a warm, professional tone. Reference specific details the guest mentioned. If the review is negative, acknowledge the concern, avoid being defensive, and offer a path to resolution. Keep it under 100 words.”

That’s it. No enterprise software. No six-figure implementation.

Guest reviews are a revenue driver. Responding to them shouldn’t take your team hours a day. Spend the time creating an experience your guests will remember.
