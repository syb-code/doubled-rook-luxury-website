What Keeps Hotels from Growing Direct Bookings

Many hotel teams already have traffic, awareness, and distribution. What they don't always have is a direct-booking system that captures demand efficiently and turns it into profitable stays.

OTA dependence stays high, cutting into margin
Paid media generates clicks, but not enough booked revenue
The booking journey has friction that quietly lowers conversion
