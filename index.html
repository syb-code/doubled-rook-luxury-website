Chapter 01

The Origin of the Rook

I'm Michael. I built my career in hotel digital marketing at Evolution Hospitality, later acquired by Aimbridge - the largest hotel operator in the world. I became the person they called when a property needed to perform immediately: low occupancy, soft direct mix, no time for a slow ramp. I developed a system for diagnosing what was broken, doubling down on what worked, and turning underperformers into top producers.

That philosophy is where the name comes from - in chess, a doubled rook is a brute-force concentration of pressure on a single line.

When the industry shut down in 2020, I moved into tech and spent five years learning ecommerce, automation, and paid media from the ground up. Now I'm back in hospitality with a sharper toolkit and the same instinct: find the lever, press harder.
